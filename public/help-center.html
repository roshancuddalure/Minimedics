Account Basics

Register, log in, verify, and recover access

Register
Register Login Email verification Forgot password
Create your account
  1. Open Register and enter your username, full name, and password.
  2. Submit the form, then sign in from Login.
  3. Use your inbox verification flow when prompted so account recovery and notifications work correctly.
Log in and stay signed in
  1. Go to Login and enter your username and password.
  2. Enable the 7-day stay-signed-in option if you are using a private device.
  3. After logging in, your dashboard becomes the main place for feed, stories, connections, and chat.
Reset or recover your password
  1. Open Forgot Password.
  2. Request a reset link or follow the token-based reset flow if you already opened the recovery email.
  3. Create a new password, confirm it, and log in again with the updated credentials.

Posting And Feed

Create posts, stories, reminders, and quizzes

Open dashboard
Share Something Stories Reminder posts Quiz mode
Post to your feed
  1. Open the dashboard and use the Share Something composer.
  2. Add your text, optional image, and choose the privacy level for the post.
  3. Publish it to make it visible to the selected audience.
Choose the right post visibility
  • Public is best for broad visibility.
  • Followers + Connections is useful for a semi-private audience.
  • Connections Only keeps the post limited to accepted connections.
Use reminder mode and quiz mode
  • Reminder mode lets you schedule a due time and optionally tag connections.
  • Tagged connections receive in-app reminders and email notifications when the reminder becomes due.
  • Quiz mode helps share learning content using a question, answer choices, and a correct option.
Create and reply to stories
  • Use Add Story from the dashboard for short text or image updates.
  • Stories auto-expire after 24 hours.
  • Viewers can like, share, and reply, and replies are sent as direct messages to the story owner.

Connections And Community

Build your network, chat, and work inside clans

Open clan area
Connection requests Direct chat Public and private clans Clan lounge
Manage connections
  • Use search, profile pages, or connection suggestions to find people.
  • Send requests, review received requests, and manage accepted, sent, and ignored states from the dashboard.
  • Accepted connections unlock direct chat and more private sharing flows.
Use direct chat
  • Start a chat from a user profile, connection list, or story reply flow.
  • You can send text and supported image attachments.
  • Chats can be minimized, reopened, and continued without leaving the dashboard.
Join or manage a clan
  • Users can have one active clan membership at a time.
  • Public clans are easier to join, while private clans require approval.
  • Inside a clan you may see activity, posts, lounge chat, invite links, requests, and role-based permissions.
Community behavior expectations
  • Keep interactions professional, relevant, and respectful.
  • Avoid abusive, misleading, or harmful medical content.
  • Clan admins and platform admins may moderate content or remove access for repeated violations.

  • Move saved posts between lists as your collections grow.

Support And Troubleshooting

Know where to ask for help and what to include

Open support center
Tickets Feature suggestions Issue reporting Status tracking
When to raise a support ticket
  • Use a ticket for login problems, bugs, broken UI, access issues, or anything that prevents normal use.
  • Write a clear subject and explain the issue in detail, including what page or action caused it.
  • Review your previously submitted tickets from the support history area.
When to submit a feature suggestion
  • Use feature suggestions for product improvements, missing workflow ideas, or better UX proposals.
  • Describe the user problem, the expected behavior, and where the feature should appear.
  • Admins can review and update the status of feature requests.
Before reporting a problem
  • Check whether the action needs an accepted connection, clan permission, or verified account state.
  • Refresh the page and retry the workflow once.
  • If the issue continues, report the exact feature name, steps tried, and the visible result.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Short answers to the things users ask most often.

Why should I verify my email?

Verification helps with account recovery and can support important notifications and trust-related platform flows.

Who can see my post?

That depends on the privacy option you selected while posting, such as public, followers plus connections, or connections only.

How long do stories stay visible?

Stories are temporary and automatically expire after 24 hours.

Who can I chat with?

Direct chat is designed around accepted connections and certain contextual flows like story replies.

Can I join more than one clan?

No. Regular users can have one active clan membership at a time.

Where do I update my bio and institute?

Open the profile editor from the dashboard or go directly to the profile page to update those fields.

How do levels and titles change?

They update automatically as your XP increases through platform activity and engagement.

Should I raise a ticket or suggest a feature?

Use a ticket for something broken or blocked. Use a feature suggestion for an improvement idea or missing capability.
