Getting Started

Register

  1. Open Register.
  2. Enter username, full name, and password.
  3. Submit the form and log in from the login page.

Login

  1. Open Login.
  2. Enter your username and password.
  3. Use "Stay logged in for 7 days" if you want a longer session.

Forgot Password

  1. Open Forgot Password.
  2. Enter username, your saved name (if present), and new password.
  3. Reset and log in with the new password.

Posting and Feed

How to Post a Feed

  1. Go to dashboard and use “Share Something”.
  2. Add text, optional image, and choose post privacy.
  3. Optional: switch to reminder mode or quiz mode.
  4. Click “Post”.

Stories

  • Stories are short updates that auto-expire in 24 hours.
  • Use “Add Story”, add text/image, and post.
  • Users can like, share, and reply to stories.

Connections, Clan, and Community

Connections: How it Works

  • Send requests from suggestions or user profile.
  • Accept or decline requests in your connections panel.
  • Accepted connections can chat and share posts directly.

Clan: Regular User Rules and Workflow

  • You can have one active clan membership at a time.
  • Public clans can be joined directly; private clans require approval.
  • Follow clan role permissions for posting, requests, and moderation features.
  • Respect medical professionalism and avoid harmful, abusive, or misleading content.
  • Repeated violations may lead to removal or account action.

Progress and Personalization

XP Points, Levels, and Titles

  • XP is earned from product activity such as posting and engagement.
  • As XP increases, your level and title update automatically.
  • Your current level/title are shown on your profile card.

Edit Profile Details

  1. Open “Edit Profile Details” from dashboard profile card.
  2. Update bio, institute, privacy options, and other fields.
  3. Save changes from the profile page.

Top Medics Leaderboard

  • Leaderboard ranks users by XP.
  • Use it to discover active contributors.

Saved Lists

  • Save useful posts from feed cards.
  • Create custom saved lists from profile page.
  • Move saved posts between lists as needed.

Theme Toggle

  • Use the “Dark/Light” button in the top action bar.
  • Your preference is saved in the browser for future visits.
